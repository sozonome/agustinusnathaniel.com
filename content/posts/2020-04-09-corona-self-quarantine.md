2020 is a bit overwhelming. So many people hate this year because of so many bad things happened since the beginning of the year, with corona pandemic being the worst. So many countries being locked down. In my country, so many people stay at home for almost a month now. So many business must close down temporarily or even collapsing. Some companies adapting to the situations, switching to work-from-home regulations. Home, a place where mostly used only to take a nap, eat, and take-a-bath, now becomes a "jail" where you must stay and do everything including working inside. 

## So, What's the Catch?
I believe in every falls, crisis, or downwards, there're always opportunities. Some schools finally start to utilize online learning to keep learning process still goes on. Some companies finally utilize more video conferencing software. TikTok, which were rising since 2019, keeps rising in this situations where people needs more entertainment at home. Instagram stories becoming a place where people show their activities and work at home because they can't go anywhere. 

At situation like this, the business which can survives are health care, basic needs, cleaning tools, IT, digital entertainment services, etc. Some restaurants and cafes closing down, some doing pre-order system. 

## How I Manage Myself during Self Quarantine
Honestly for me, staying at home is not really hard because I spent most of my life at home. Studying, working, I do it mostly at home. So I don't find so much problems being at home.
